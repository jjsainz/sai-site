Flexible offshore staffing without lock-ins or hidden fees

In New Zealand, many frontline support roles now sit at around NZD $51,000 per year before you even add recruitment, onboarding, and overheads. In Australia, similar roles often start from about AUD $58,000. Once you factor in Super or KiwiSaver, leave, workspace, equipment, and HR, the fully loaded cost can easily reach $75,000–$80,000 or more per year.

Through our vetted offshore providers, an equivalent team member typically lands at around NZD $35,000 or AUD $30,000 per year, fully loaded. Facilities, equipment, local HR, and compliance are included. You pay a clear monthly fee, with no setup charges and the ability to reduce headcount or cancel a role with 30 days’ notice.

The outcome is simple: you keep or improve service levels while freeing up budget to invest in growth, product, or your onshore team.

A zero-friction pathway that actually works for AU/NZ businesses

Every business operates differently — workflows, communication style, culture, and risk appetite. That’s why everything starts with a quick suitability check. It’s a short, practical conversation to confirm that offshore support will genuinely work for your structure before any introductions are made.

When there’s a fit, we introduce you to a vetted offshore provider whose strengths align with your needs. They handle sourcing, testing, shortlisting, and presenting candidates — so you only speak with people who are likely to succeed in your environment.

Hire and manage the way that suits you

All hiring and HR — sourcing, assessments, contracts, payroll, and local compliance — is handled by the offshore provider. You choose how hands-on you want to be operationally, depending on what works best for your business.

  • Full control: Your offshore team can report to you daily, just like in-house staff.
  • Outcome-focused: Set KPIs and let the provider manage day-to-day operations.
  • Flexible hiring: Interview every candidate yourself, or delegate selection if you prefer a more hands-off approach.

No matter your preference, you keep visibility and flexibility — while the provider handles the administration and HR workload.

Why businesses choose this pathway

The goal is simple: reduce overhead and eliminate friction without compromising standards. AU/NZ businesses choose this model because it delivers capability and flexibility that traditional hiring struggles to match.

  • No long-term contracts: Roles can be reduced or cancelled with 30 days’ notice.
  • Transparent costs: One clear monthly fee with no setup charges or hidden add-ons.
  • Better alignment: Culture fit, communication style, and expectations are validated upfront — not discovered months into the relationship.

The result is a low-risk, high-performance pathway that helps teams scale while keeping costs predictable and manageable.

Built for teams of any size

Many businesses start with a single VA or support role to take pressure off the core team. Others use this pathway to build multi-layered offshore capability across customer service, collections, admin, back-office, or specialist functions.

Whether you're hiring one person or one hundred, the approach stays the same: keep risk low, keep your options open, and help you scale confidently while others struggle with overhead and rising costs.
