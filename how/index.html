Approach

How I work

Engagements are calm, thoughtful, and grounding. People often arrive with something unclear and leave with clearer thinking and a simpler path forward.

What to expect

Working with JJ can feel like working with a great coach. The aim is to create the conditions for clear thinking. Through careful listening and a few well-placed questions, complexity begins to settle and reality becomes easier to see. Conversations are open and thoughtful, giving people space to think out loud. Once the situation is properly understood, a small number of simple actions often create meaningful change.

Most conversations take place in person in Christchurch, New Zealand. I also work remotely with clients across the North Island and overseas. Sessions can take place in Spanish if preferred.

Engagement options & pricing

These are the main ways we can work together.

  • Clarity session

    A focused 90-minute conversation to surface priorities, risks, and immediate next steps. Often used when an important decision needs clearer thinking, or when something feels “off” and deserves careful perspective.

    From NZD 650 per session.
  • Advisory sessions

    A three-hour working session for more complex situations. Useful when exploring a strategic decision, thinking through a partnership, or working through a difficult leadership challenge.

    From NZD 1,300 per session.
  • Strategic consulting

    Short, precise input when something urgent or specialised arises. Often used for a second opinion, negotiation preparation, or reviewing an important decision.

    From NZD 450 per hour.
  • Strategic projects

    Defined scope for work that requires more than conversation alone. Examples include reviewing an operating model, selecting a partner or vendor, or supporting a significant strategic shift.

    From NZD 10,000 per project.
  • Ongoing partnership

    A light advisory partnership for ongoing thinking and accountability. Regular conversations help leaders stay clear as decisions, risks, and opportunities evolve.

    From NZD 5,000 per month.
