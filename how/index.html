Approach

How I work

Engagements are calm, thoughtful, and grounding. People often arrive with something weighing on them and leave with clearer thinking, renewed energy, and a sense that the path forward is simpler than it first appeared.

What to expect

I focus on understanding the real problem first. Through careful listening and a few well placed questions, complexity becomes clearer. I do not default to stiff boardroom meetings. I prefer honest conversations where people can think out loud. Once the situation is understood properly, a few simple actions can often create meaningful change.

I work remotely or in person in Christchurch, New Zealand. I can also work with you in Spanish if that is easier.
