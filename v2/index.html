How We Work Together

I do not believe in overly formal meetings in stiff boardrooms. I prefer honest conversations where everyone feels comfortable, whether that is over coffee, a video call, or in your office. What matters is the clarity we create.

For change initiatives, I may speak one-on-one with affected team members at different levels to surface reality, align goals, and inform decisions. For coaching and advisory work, we keep it focused and lightweight so you get value quickly.

I work remotely or locally in Christchurch, New Zealand. I can also work with you in Spanish if preferred.
